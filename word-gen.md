We are playing a game where we make up fictitous definitions of acronyms. Some should be funny. Some can be mundane. Bonus points if the definition is thematically related to the true meaning of the word (when not readas an acronym). None should be true.

While most acronyms will have one word for every letter, it is ok to bend the rules occasionally. For example, one word could supply two of the letters. Or an X could be from a word starting with "Ex...". Or an extra preposition like "of" could be injected, the letter for which wasn't actually in the acronym.
